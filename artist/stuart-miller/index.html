Considered composition, a natural crafting of light and a strong visual narrative are all trademarks of Stuart Miller’s photography and motion.

Having graduated with a Bachelor of Design in Photograph, Stuart’s body of work extends from portrait and documentary work to advertising, still life and lifestyle photography. He loves nothing more than capturing engaging moments that convey a range of human emotions and stories, while bringing soul and colour to his still-life work.
