Bonnie Coumbe is a Sydney based food photographer known for her clean and elegant approach to food photography. She enjoys working with natural ingredients in their raw form, always focused on capturing food to reveal its optimum appetite appeal.

Bonnie's photographic style is characterised by simplicity and the use of light to create depth and dimension. She is often exploring colour, texture and shape, combined with carefully crafted compositions that allow the food to take centre stage.

We are here on the land of the Gadigal people and acknowledge them as the traditional owners of country. We recognise their continuing connection to country and we pay our respects to elders past, present and emerging.
