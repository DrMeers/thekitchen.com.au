Rarely without a camera in hand, Andrew Cowen captures the ordinary and extraordinary elements of daily life elevating the mundane to the wild and bringing the surreal back down to earth.

Andrew documents the intricate details of his environment and the people in his life through portraiture, still life and atmospheric landscape work. His portraits genuine and raw, create a familiarity in his subjects and their individual stories - no matter how outlandish. With his ability to capture unassuming beauty, Andrew transforms even the most ordinary subject into an artistic vision.
