About

The Kitchen has grown organically from humble beginnings. Founded in 2000 from a one bedroom apartment in Potts Point Sydney, today the agency is at the forefront of creative artist management, representing some of Australiaâ€™s most well-respected and dynamic image makers.

At The Kitchen we strive for perfection from the ground up, ensuring that every facet of production is handled with integrity, passion and a fine attention to detail. At our core we believe that photography is a love affair with life and we aim to uphold that ethos every step of the way.

We manage a handpicked selection of Australia's most innovative and driven photographers. Our artists have a combined wealth of creative and industry experience and most importantly, they are all a pleasure to work with.

Our aim is to assist, motivate and encourage our artists in achieving their creative and commercial goals whilst ensuring we consistently meet our client's expectations in terms of creativity, production quality and service.
