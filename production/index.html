Good production is so much more than the sum of its parts. It requires the obvious: strong organisational skills, an extensive and reliable network, tenacity and agility, collaboration, and a problem-solving approach. Beyond this, good production is marked out by a profound understanding of and appreciation for the end product.

With 20 years of experience decoding image-making, The Kitchen has an invested passion for photography, film and the processes behind the craft. It's how we've earned our stellar reputation.

We understand that no two production briefs come the same, so we have a scalable approach. Whether you are a photographer, a creative agency or another production company, we operate as a production partner, able to slot into whatever the scenario requires. We are happy to be involved at all levels; from end-to end production, art-buying to final file delivery, or if its just a portion, such as location scouting, casting, or on-set line production, we are here to help.

ENVIRONMENT POLICY

The Kitchen is a committed environmentally responsible organisation. We actively look to reduce, reuse and recycle and we committed to promoting our environmental policies and ethos. We aim to:

  • Cease ** the use of disposable plastic waters bottles and coffee cups on set.
  • Reduce the amount of landfill we produce by providing washable and reusable crockery and cutlery on set.
  • Be a paperless office and use electronic copies of all production documents, call sheets, briefs and treatments.
  • Actively reduce waste through our recycling management program.
  • Use energy efficient equipment and recycled or biodegradable products.
  • Divert waste from landfill by offering leftover props, sets and wardrobe to local charities.

** Due to the COVID-19 pandemic we are currently unable to uphold some of our environmental policies on a comprehensive basis. We are applying the recommendations of the Chief Heath Office and are currently using single use water bottles and coffee cups to ensure the health and safety of everyone on set.

COVID-19 POLICY

The Kitchen is a COVID safe business. We are offering our clients live streaming packages to limit the number of people on set. This enables clients to review shoots remotely as they happen. We also offer a similar package on-set with multiple screens and a continuous feed to ensure people are able to maintain a safe physical distance. Contact us for more details on our COVID policy and procedures.
