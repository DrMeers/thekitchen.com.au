Deep sea diving with Autore Pearls' campaign on location in Indonesia by Tim Swallow.

"When I was offered to shoot the Autore campaign on Lombok and the Gili Islands in Indonesia I was so excited. The trip through the island felt like I was on a 1970s expedition. Life there is slow paced and everyone smiles a lot. I arrived at the showroom which was literally floating above a beautiful coral reef lagoon. The water is crystal clear and the farm is situated just off the coast. After diving to depths of -25m to capture the Pearl divers harvesting, we headed back to the farm to process the catch and shoot portraits with the workers. Later that afternoon we headed out to the Gilli's to shoot the campaign. The sunset was truly stunning that evening and we shot till dark, I returned to land pleasantly exhausted."

We are here on the land of the Gadigal people and acknowledge them as the traditional owners of country. We recognise their continuing connection to country and we pay our respects to elders past, present and emerging.
