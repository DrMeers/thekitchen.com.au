Recently, Stuart Miller went to Parkes in Regional NSW to shoot the annual Elvis Festival held every year in the second week of January around Elvis' birthday. The images were published in The Guardian with an accompany story written by Katie Cunningham.

Read the article in The Guardian here. 

An image from this series is a finalist in this year's Australian Life competition held by the City of Sydney. Work from Australian Life finalists will be on exhibition in Hyde Park from September 15 to October 9, 2022.
