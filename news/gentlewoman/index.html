Andrew Cowen shoots Emma Balfour in Bottega Veneta SS23 in a new editorial for The Gentlewoman, issue No. 27.

 

"FAR: Emma Balfour – the epochal 1990s model – was photographed at Little Forest, her family’s weekend retreat two hours’ drive from Sydney. The home – a shed with a mezzanine level containing two bedrooms, and a downstairs divided into a work area and bathroom and a large living area that has double-height ceilings – is as enduring as Emma herself. “Touch wood,” she jests,⁠ “but this house is bulletproof.”⁠
⁠
⁠Here, she is wearing all BOTTEGA VENETA for Spring/Summer 2023.⁠"

- The Gentlewoman
