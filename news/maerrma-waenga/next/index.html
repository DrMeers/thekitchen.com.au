Commissioned by Living Magazine, Corriere della Sera, Mattia Panunzio takes a closer look at the forefront of design and architecture in his adopted Sydney home, visiting the studios and workshops of makers and designers across furniture, textiles, sculpture and industrial design.

 

Read the full story, written by Barbara Gallucci, in this month's issue of Living. And to see more of the Sydney-based creatives featured, click through below:
